COMING TO THEATERS CHRISTMAS 2024
In the upcoming political mystery thriller "Aspersion," the stakes are high as President Chase Lewis desperately tries to secure his reelection. However, he faces a formidable opponent in the charismatic Peter Turner. After a shocking debate loss, Lewis's desperation transforms into ruthless determination, leading him to orchestrate Turner's demise. As the government officials conspire to keep the assassination a secret and make it look like an accident, Jimmy Jenkins, the president's most trusted agent, grapples with the morality of this act. Sonny, Peter's son quickly plunges into a deep depression. However, Sonny's life takes an unexpected turn when a mysterious stranger delivers crucial information about his father's death. Fueled by this revelation, Sonny embarks on a perilous journey for justice and revenge against those responsible for his father's murder, navigating a treacherous world of political intrigue and covert operations. "Aspersion" weaves a tale of betrayal, courage, suspense, and the relentless pursuit of truth in the face of political machinations. Scroll down to learn about the 4 main characters.
