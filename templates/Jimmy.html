Jimmy Jenkins's backstory
Jimmy Jenkins, a reserved individual from northern California, possesses a deep respect for the law and an unwavering commitment to the president. Raised by strict parents, he adapted to never question and blindly follow rules, which carried over to his career as a government official. However, as time goes on, Jimmy faces a crossroads, the habit of unquestioning obedience is on the brink of change, challenging the very foundations of his childhood and professional life.
