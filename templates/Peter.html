Peter Turner's backstory
Born and raised in Virginia, Peter Turner exudes a perpetual energy and confidence that defines his character. Despite his energetic persona, Peter dedicated a substantial amount of his time to political pursuits, somewhat neglecting the challenges his son, Sonny, was facing. Born into a family of politicians, Peter was molded by the political landscape that surrounded him. His parents, significant figures in the political world, played a significant role in shaping his worldview.
